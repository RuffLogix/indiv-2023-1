Jae Oh's Restaurant

Jae Oh restaurant operates as usual every day from 16:30 to 00:00. For more information, contact 064-118-5888.

Jae Oh, the creator of the famous Oho Mala, has gained worldwide fame with a fantastic recipe. The Oho Mala at this restaurant is a must-try, and it's advisable to come early as the place tends to be crowded every day. Besides the famous Oho Mala, the menu includes other delicious dishes such as:

- Braised Duck (100-200 Baht)
- Spicy Grilled Mushroom Salad (60 Baht)
- Shrimp marinated in fish sauce (150 Baht)
- Boiled Razor Clams (100 Baht)
- Salmon Salad (300 Baht/550 Baht)
- Oho Mala with minced pork (150 Baht)
- Crispy Pork Face (150 Baht)
- Seafood Oho Mala (220 Baht)

Jae Oh has been awarded the Michelin Guide Bib Gourmand in 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022. The restaurant is located on Chaturamit Road, Rong Mueang Sub-district, Pathumwan District, Bangkok 10330. Contact them at 064-118-5888. Open every day from 16:30 to 00:00. Cuisine type: Thai.

Jae Oh, or Jae Oh, Khao Tom Ped, is a legendary Thai-Chinese restaurant known for its street food style. It has been famous for its delicious dishes among the people of the three districts and has been recommended by the Michelin Guide in 2020. The restaurant has been open since the time of Khong and Amma, and although the menu used to be limited to basics like Braised Duck, Pork Stomach, and Braised Duck, it has expanded to include more diverse dishes such as Crispy Pork, Gyoza, Stir-Fried Water Spinach, and many more.
