"ต้าเสี่ยวหม่าล่า" (Tasiaw Mala Hotpot)

Tasiaw Mala Hotpot is a Mala hotpot restaurant located on Bantadthong Road. The restaurant operates daily from 17:00 to 00:00. For inquiries, contact 081-838-2788. Positioned opposite Chulalongkorn University, the restaurant is a hotspot for those seeking bold and spicy flavors, especially during late-night hours, as it remains open until midnight. The Mala sauce used in the dishes is an authentic recipe from China, providing a rich, fiery, and aromatic taste. Customers can customize the spice level according to their preference. The menu features Mala grilled skewers with a variety of options, including pork, seafood, and assorted vegetables.

Address: 1366 Bantadthong Road, Wang Mai Subdistrict, Pathum Wan District, Bangkok 10330.
Contact: 081-838-2788
Operating hours: Daily from 17:00 to 00:00
Cuisine: Mala (Spicy Hotpot)

Key Features:

Authentic Mala hotpot with customizable spice levels.
Located near Chulalongkorn University, making it easily accessible.
Extended operating hours until midnight.
Mala sauce crafted from an original Chinese recipe, offering a full-bodied and spicy flavor.
Diverse menu with Mala grilled skewers featuring pork, seafood, and various vegetables.
Customers can request their preferred spice level to suit their taste preferences.
