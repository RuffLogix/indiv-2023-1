"Engchuan Chufang Restaurant"

Engchuan Chufang Restaurant operates regularly from 11:30 AM to 9:00 PM, closed on Wednesdays. For inquiries, please call 092-362-4999. Located within the Dragon Town project, this Chinese restaurant and cafe, known as Engchuan Chufang or ร้านเอ่งฉ้วน ฉูฝาง, offers a Chinese-style ambiance that is visually appealing and perfect for capturing picturesque moments. The menu features a variety of single-dish Chinese meals, including delicious options such as seafood suki, crispy Taiwanese-style chicken noodles, crispy chicken suki, Hong Kong-style milk tea, and fragrant oolong tea.

Address: Dragon Town Project, Soi Wang Mai, Pathum Wan District, Bangkok 10330.
Contact: 092-362-4999
Operating hours: 11:30 AM to 9:00 PM, closed on Wednesdays.
Types of cuisine: Chinese, Order rice and curry (single-dish meals, rice and curry), Cafe.
