Jie Puy Phochana Restaurant

Jie Puy Phochana Restaurant operates regularly every day from 11:00 AM to 12:00 AM. For further inquiries, please call 081-513-1163.

Jie Puy Phochana is a well-known custom-order restaurant in the area, widely recognized for its delectable offerings and air-conditioned setting. The restaurant's late-night service makes it a favored spot for frequent visits. The menu encompasses a variety of custom-order dishes, including the popular Kuay Teow noodles served with rice soup. Notable selections consist of Steamed Grouper with Lime, Oyster Sauce Sautéed Shrimp and Young Bamboo Shoots, Pickled Crab Eggs, Red Curry Stir-fried Vegetables, and Crispy Pork with Water Spinach. Prices typically range between 100 to 200 Baht.

Address: 1856 Bantadthong Road, Wangmai Sub-district, Pathumwan District, Bangkok.
Contact: 081-513-1163
Operating Hours: Every day from 11:00 AM to 12:00 AM.
Type of Cuisine: Order rice and curry (Single dish, made-to-order, rice curry)
