Tubtim Restaurant

Tubtim Restaurant operates regularly from 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM (closed on Sundays). For inquiries, call 090-889-3007 / 061-759-1007.

Tubtim Restaurant is another establishment known for its signature dish, Kao Kluk Kapi (shrimp paste fried rice), which ranks among Thailand's best. Inside the restaurant, you'll find a variety of delicious options such as deep-fried pork with dipping sauce, pork bun, spaghetti with minced meat sauce, and stir-fried beef noodles. The menu combines both Thai and international dishes, providing customers with a diverse selection. Prices start at 45 Baht and are quite reasonable for the tasty offerings.

Address: 1771 Bantadthong Road, Wang Mai Sub-district, Pathum Wan District, Bangkok.
Contact: 090-889-3007 / 061-759-1007
Operating Hours: 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM, closed on Sundays.
Type of Cuisine: Thai

Tubtim Restaurant, operating from 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM (closed on Sundays), offers a menu featuring the renowned Kao Kluk Kapi (shrimp paste fried rice) that holds a distinguished position in Thai cuisine. The menu seamlessly blends both Thai and international dishes, allowing customers to choose from a diverse array of offerings. Prices start at 45 Baht and are considered quite reasonable for the delectable fare.
