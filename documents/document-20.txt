Lek Mai Lek Restaurant
Lek Mai Lek Restaurant operates regularly every day from 11:00 to 21:00.
For additional inquiries, please contact 095-554-2681 / 091-886-7168.
When craving a variety of delicious papaya salads, you must visit Lek Mai Lek. Located on Chula Soi 6, this restaurant offers Thai, Lao, and pickled crab egg papaya salads. Customers who enjoy spicy flavors are in for a treat, and prices generally range from 80 to 250 Baht per dish.

If you're driving, you can park on both Chula Soi 4 and Chula Soi 6. Parking on Soi 4 is slightly closer, and you can bring your parking ticket to the restaurant to get one hour of free parking. Alternatively, if you take the BTS and exit at the National Stadium station, then walk towards the Chaloem Phao intersection, you'll find the restaurant.

Address: Stadium One, Soi Chula Soi 6, Bantadthong Road, Wang Mai Sub-district, Pathumwan District, Bangkok 10330.
Contact Numbers: 02-163-4668.
Opening Hours: Every day from 11:00 to 21:00.
Shop Type: Som Tum (Papaya Salad)
