Jae Kiang Restaurant

Jae Kiang Restaurant operates regularly from 16:00 to 23:00.

For more information, contact 02-050-1086 / 086-884-4352.

Jae Kiang Restaurant (Old Bridge Yellow) has relocated from Yellow Bridge to Chula 6 (along Banthat Thong Road). The restaurant focuses on menu items such as duck, goose, and a variety of customizable dishes. If you enjoy steamed sea crab with soy sauce, note that it is available only on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays. The menu starts at 50 baht, and you can expect a diverse selection to continually satisfy your taste buds.

Address:
679 Banthat Thong Road (between Chula Soi 4 and 6),
Wang Mai, Pathum Wan, Bangkok 10330

Contact Number: 086-884-4352 / 02-050-1086

Business Hours: Every day from 16:00 to 23:00

Type of Shop: Order rice and curry (single-dish, customizable) and legendary duck noodle shop

Introduction:

Jae Kiang Noodle Shop, a legendary establishment from Yellow Bridge, has been serving its renowned dishes near Suan Luang for nearly two decades. The flavors remain consistent, and the aroma of the wok lingers in the air every time you order a stir-fried dish. Today, we're indulging in boiled rice, accompanied by additional offerings such as Yen Ta Fo hot pot, clay pot-roasted duck wings, sunflower sprout stir-fry with red chili paste, baby corn stir-fry with oyster sauce, and Hong Kong-style braised noodles. Satisfy your appetite with these delightful dishes.
