"Angpao Mala Hotpot"

Angpao Mala Hotpot is a newly opened restaurant located on Bantadthong Road. Operating hours are Monday to Friday from 13:00 to 23:30 and Saturday to Sunday from 12:00 to 23:30. For more information, contact 063-885-3880. The restaurant is known for its authentic Sichuan-style Mala hotpot, featuring rich and intensely flavorful broths that appeal to Mala enthusiasts. Besides the signature Mala soup, other options include Tomato Soup, Collagen Pork Bone Soup, and Tom Yum Soup. Recommended dishes include Slender and Tender Beef Slices, Kurubota Pork Neck, Duck Intestines, Fried Stuffed Tofu Tubes, Grilled Pork Intestines, and Skewered Shell-on Shrimp with Glutinous Rice Dumplings.
