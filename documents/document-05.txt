"เสน่ห์ลาบก้อย" (Sen Lao Koi)

Sen Lao Koi is a popular Northeastern (Isaan) restaurant located on Soi Chula 20, behind I'm Park, near the 100th Anniversary Park in Bangkok. The restaurant operates daily from 16:00 to 00:00, except on Mondays. For inquiries, contact 084-239-5692.

Known for its delicious and spicy Northeastern cuisine, Sen Lao Koi attracts customers in the Bantadthong-Chulalongkorn area. The restaurant offers a wide selection of Isaan dishes with authentic flavors and traditional Isaan style. Signature dishes include Somtum (papaya salad), Puplarah (fermented fish paste), Koi Larb (raw minced pork), Grilled Pork Neck, and various dipping sauces. Each dish is reasonably priced, ranging from approximately 50 to 100 Baht.

Address: 11 Chula 20, Wang Mai Subdistrict, Pathum Wan District, Bangkok 10330.
Contact: 084-239-5692
Operating hours: 16:00-00:00 (Closed on Sundays)
Cuisine: Thainortheast (Northeastern/Isaan)

Key Features:

Authentic Isaan cuisine with bold and spicy flavors.
Located on Soi Chula 20, easily accessible near Chulalongkorn University.
Extended operating hours until midnight, except on Mondays.
Signature dish "Koi Larb" is made with quality, freshly cut raw pork and seasoned with a special spice blend.
Diverse menu with other flavorful dishes such as Somtum, Grilled Pork Neck, and spicy soups.
Customer reviews praise the deliciousness of the raw minced pork and the rich, well-balanced flavors of the dishes.
Prices start at around 80 Baht, making it an affordable choice for quality Isaan cuisine enthusiasts.
